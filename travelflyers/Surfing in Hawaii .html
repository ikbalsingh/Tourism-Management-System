Surfing in Hawaii

Kaaawa, HI, United States

Having fun in the sun, and surfing the big waves of Hawaii.

I enjoyed the big waves of Oahu, Hawaii. The water falls on the island are the best thing I've seen. The food is irresistible, with king crab and shrimp, and with a side of salad. The locals were nice, and the island has a very nice and clean environment. The best part was probably the crystal clear beaches, with the sharp upward cliffs next to the sea. Definitively a place to come and see!
