Peru Adventure

Peru

Adventuring around Peru, South America. Visiting ancient ruins, and exploring the wide variety of foods Peru has to offer.

It was terrific to travel this past month into the middle of the Amazonian forest to discover one of the 7 Wonders of the World. It is in a remote location so understandable why it was not discovered until just a little over a century ago. This wonder has been around and stood the test of time for centuries yet only discovered in the 20th Century. The ingenuity of the ancient Incas are still raising questions that modern day scientists do not know the answers to yet. What an ancient marvel in modern day society.
