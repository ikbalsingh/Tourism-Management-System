CityByCitizen aims to help the tourists to become travelers.

Most of us agree that to truly experience a city, we must immerse ourselves in the local culture and habits. CityByCitizen connects tourists seeking local experiences with citizens who want to share their passion for their city.

The citizens are independent. They design their own experiences according to their interests and passions.
